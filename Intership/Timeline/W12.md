**Absolute Error in COLMAP scale
$$
\text{err}_{\text{abs}}(\mathbf{t}_{colmap}, \mathbf{t}_{estimated})
=
\left\|
\mathbf{t}_{colmap}
-
\left(
\frac{\|\mathbf{t}_{colmap}\|}{\|\mathbf{t}_{estimated}\|} \cdot \mathbf{t}_{estimated}
\right)
\right\|
$$

- **Range**: (0 $\le$ $\text{err}_{\text{abs}} < \infty$)
- Increases with misalignment in **scale** and **direction**.

**Relative Angular Error between unit vectors**

$$ 
\theta(t_{colmap}, t_{estimated}) 
= \arccos \left( 
\frac{t_{colmap}}{\|t_{colmap}\|} \cdot \frac{t_{estimated}}{\|t_{estimated}\|} 
\right) 
$$

- **Range:** $0^\circ \le \theta \le 180^\circ$
- $0^\circ \rightarrow$ same direction; $180^\circ \rightarrow$ opposite direction; $90^\circ \rightarrow$ orthogonal.
