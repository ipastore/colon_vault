### Memory Calculation for Tensor Dimensions

#### Detailed Calculation

##### For 1, 60, 84, 60, 84, 256 tensor:
1. Total elements = 1 Ă— 60 Ă— 84 Ă— 60 Ă— 84 Ă— 256 = 6,481,092,608

#### Why is the Reduction So Dramatic?

The key insight is that the tensor has **quadratic scaling** with respect to image dimensions because it represents all possible pairs of points between two images:
