## Key Parts Explained:

1. **The `modify_poses_with_custom_results` Function:**
    
    - Constructs a graph of connected images from your custom matches
    - Starts from a reference image (using its COLMAP ground truth pose)
    - Uses breadth-first search to propagate poses through the graph
    - Composes relative poses to get absolute poses in COLMAP's coordinate system
2. **Visualization:**
    
    - Ground truth COLMAP poses shown in blue
    - Your custom matcher's poses shown in red
    - 3D points from COLMAP shown for context
3. **Handling Relative vs. Absolute Poses:**
    
    - The function converts your relative poses (camera 2 with respect to camera 1) to absolute poses (camera in world coordinates)
    - This is crucial for meaningful comparison with COLMAP's ground truth
